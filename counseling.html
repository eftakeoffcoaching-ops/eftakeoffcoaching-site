Mental Health Counseling

You don't have to figure this out alone.

Does the worry ever really turn off, or does it just get quieter for a while?

Do the people who used to feel close feel further away lately?

Is there something, old or recent, that you're carrying around without much support?

What we can work on together

  • Anxiety that won't quiet down, from a constant hum to full panic
  • Depression and low mood, even when nothing feels "wrong" but nothing feels good either
  • The emotional side of ADHD: shame spirals, shutdowns, the stuff coaching alone doesn't reach
  • Perfectionism that's stopped helping and started costing you
  • Adjusting to life abroad, whether you just landed or you've been here for years
  • Work and family stress, especially with your support system a time zone away
  • Grief, big transitions, relationship strain
  • Wanting to understand yourself better or break a pattern you're tired of

When I'll point you somewhere else

  • In crisis, suicidal, or at risk of harming yourself or someone else: go to your nearest emergency room or call local emergency services
  • Need medication: I can't prescribe, but I'll gladly work alongside your psychiatrist
  • Active psychosis, mania, or a primary substance use issue: you'll need a higher level of care than I provide, though I can support you alongside it
How I Work

An approach built around what's actually going on.

A space without judgment

You won't be rushed, dismissed, or told your reaction is too much. Whatever you bring, I take it seriously.

Sessions follow what's actually going on

There's no fixed script. What we focus on can shift as things change, that's normal, not a sign anything's off track.

What you bring here stays here. It isn't shared with employers, schools, or family, and it isn't something to feel awkward about, even if that's not always how it goes in this part of the world.

Sessions are available by telehealth or in person in Shenzhen, 50 minutes each.

Currently working toward clinical licensure and practicing under supervision.
